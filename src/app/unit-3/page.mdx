# Unit 3: Essay Practice

Welcome to the third unit of Advanced Prep! This unit is designed to help you practice and refine your essay writing skills necessary for excelling in the Advanced AET admissions process. We will focus on enhancing your ability to present ideas clearly and reason logically, as well as articulating the impact of your arguments from different perspectives. Let's continue your journey to success! {{ className: 'lead' }}

## Lesson 3.1: Presentation/Reasoning

### Practicing Essay Writing and Presentation Skills

- **Objective:** Organize ideas clearly and address the topic directly in essay writing.
- **Activity:** Practice writing essays with clear organization and well-reasoned arguments.

### Step-by-Step Guide:

1. **Organizing Ideas:**

   - Learn techniques for organizing ideas in a clear and logical manner.
   - Focus on addressing the topic directly and providing detailed explanations.
   - **Integration of Rubric Criteria:**
     - **Presentation/Reasoning:** Present ideas in a clear, organized manner that directly addresses the topic.

2. **Presenting Arguments:**

   - Practice constructing logical and persuasive arguments.
   - Learn how to present counterarguments effectively.
   - **Integration of Rubric Criteria:**
     - **Presentation/Reasoning:** Present detailed and clear descriptions of meaningful possibilities. Address and pursue counterarguments.

## Lesson 3.2: Point of View/Perspective

### Developing and Articulating Perspectives

- **Objective:** Describe the impact of your solution on a wide range of audiences.
- **Activity:** Practice writing essays that highlight the significance of your arguments for different audiences.

### Step-by-Step Guide:

1. **Describing Impact:**

   - Learn techniques for articulating the impact of your solutions on various audiences.
   - Focus on describing significant impacts clearly.
   - **Integration of Rubric Criteria:**
     - **Point of View/Perspective:** Describe the impact on a wide range of audiences or where the solution would have a major impact.

2. **Highlighting Significance:**

   - Practice emphasizing the importance of your arguments in your writing.
   - Learn how to make your solutions relevant to different groups.
   - **Integration of Rubric Criteria:**
     - **Point of View/Perspective:** Highlight the significant impact of your arguments on relevant audiences.

## End Goals

- **Develop strong presentation and reasoning skills.**
- **Enhance your ability to organize and present your arguments effectively.**
- **Articulate the impact of your solutions on various audiences.**

By the end of this unit, you'll have refined your essay writing skills, preparing you to excel in the Advanced AET admissions process and present yourself as a compelling candidate.

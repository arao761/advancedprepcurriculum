## Curriculum Overview

Our curriculum is structured to provide a comprehensive learning experience, starting with understanding the admissions process and developing core skills. As you progress, we will delve into advanced essay writing and final preparation strategies to ensure you are fully prepared for the admissions test.

## Objectives

- Master the Advanced AET admissions process and its criteria.
- Develop critical reading, writing, and scientific writing skills.
- Enhance problem-solving and analytical skills.
- Gain practical experience through essay practice with real prompts.
- Prepare comprehensively for the admissions test with final tips and a mock test.

## Final Preparation

Your journey will culminate in final preparation: learning last-minute tips and strategies, taking a mock admissions test, and reviewing your performance. This is your opportunity to refine your skills and ensure you are fully prepared for the Advanced AET admissions process.

Stay excited, and let’s embark on this transformative learning experience!
